About Us

Managing Directors : Joseph Tharayil & Reni Joseph Tharayil

Cheif Technology Officer : Martin Joseph Tharayil

Golden City Jewellery was established 26 years ago in Velur with a vision to offer high-quality jewellery and trusted service to our valued customers. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for excellence, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. Our showroom features an ever-growing collection of beautiful gold ornaments, including necklaces, bangles, rings, earrings, and bridal jewellery designed to suit every occasion. With a commitment to quality, trust, and elegance, Golden City Jewellery continues to serve generations of customers with designs that blend tradition and modern style.
